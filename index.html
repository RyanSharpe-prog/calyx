FAQ

Common questions.

Is this the same as Ozempic or Wegovy?

Calyx uses the same active ingredient found in brand-name GLP-1 medications, sourced from a licensed, regulated U.S. pharmacy — at a significantly lower cost.

Is it injectable or a pill?

GLP-1 medication is administered as a once-weekly subcutaneous injection — the same delivery method used by Ozempic and Wegovy. Our team walks you through everything on your intro call.

How quickly will I see results?

Most people notice reduced appetite within the first few weeks. Meaningful weight loss typically becomes visible within 8–12 weeks, with continued progress over time.

What's included in the $199/month?

Your monthly cost covers medication, shipping, and ongoing support. Pricing varies slightly by dose — your intro call will confirm exact pricing for your plan.

Who is Calyx right for?

Calyx is designed for adults who have struggled to lose weight through diet and exercise alone and are looking for a clinically-backed, affordable alternative to brand-name GLP-1.

What happens on the free intro call?

We'll learn about your goals, answer all your questions, and walk you through how the program works. Zero pressure — just a real conversation to see if Calyx is right for you.
