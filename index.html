FAQ

Common questions.

Is this the same as Ozempic or Wegovy?

Yes — Calyx uses the same active ingredient (semaglutide) found in brand-name GLP-1 medications, sourced from a licensed U.S. compounding pharmacy at a significantly lower cost.

Is it injectable or a pill?

GLP-1 is administered as a once-weekly subcutaneous self-injection — the same method used by Ozempic and Wegovy. It's simpler than it sounds, and our team walks you through everything.

Who prescribes my medication?

Calyx works with a network of independent licensed providers who review your intake and issue your prescription. All included — no extra fees, no office visits.

How do I claim the switching promotion?

Use code SWITCH100 at checkout. Valid for members currently on 5mg dose or higher from another GLP-1 program. $100 off your first month only.

How does the pound-dropping rewards program work?

Your weight loss milestones are verified by our concierge team. Rewards are issued as account credits. Maximum total reward is $200 per member over a minimum 90-day membership. Terms apply.

Can I use Affirm, Klarna, or Afterpay?

Yes — Calyx supports Care Now Pay Later via Affirm, Klarna, and Afterpay. Split your membership into manageable payments with zero interest options available.
