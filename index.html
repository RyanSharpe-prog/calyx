FAQ

Common questions.

What's the difference between Semaglutide and Tirzepatide?

Semaglutide is the active ingredient in Ozempic and Wegovy. Tirzepatide is a dual GLP-1/GIP agonist — the active ingredient in Zepbound and Mounjaro — and is clinically shown to produce greater weight loss. Our providers will help you choose based on your goals and health history.

Is it injectable or a pill?

GLP-1 is administered as a once-weekly subcutaneous self-injection — the same method as Ozempic and Wegovy. It sounds intimidating but most members are comfortable within the first week. Our team walks you through everything.

Who prescribes my medication?

Calyx works with a network of independent licensed providers who review your intake, labs, and health history before issuing your prescription. All included — no extra fees, no office visit required.

How do the baseline labs work?

After signing up, you'll receive an order for baseline labs through our Labcorp or Quest network. Visit your nearest location, results come back in a few days, and your provider reviews them before prescribing. It's all coordinated for you.

How does the switching promotion work?

Use code SWITCH100 at checkout. Valid for members currently on 5mg dose or higher from another GLP-1 provider. $100 off your first month only. Your provider will review your current plan and transition you smoothly.

How do the pound-dropping rewards work?

Milestones are verified by our concierge team and paid as account credits toward future months. Total rewards are capped at $200 per member over a minimum 90-day membership. The tiers are $5/lb (1–10 lbs), $10/lb (10–20 lbs), and $5/lb (20–30 lbs) — adding up to $200 maximum.
